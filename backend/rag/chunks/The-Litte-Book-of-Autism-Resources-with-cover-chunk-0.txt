Forward Thanks to Marty Murphy and Chuck Hartseil for your help and support in editing and updating the Autism Resource book. Marty for spending countless hours researching information and Chuck for picking up everything I ignore when focused on a project. Many thanks to the pioneers in our autism community for believing that your vision and hard work could bring about the changes needed in quality of life, education and awareness. Virginia Moody, Kari Sandhaas, Karla Doepke, Stacy Bock, and Nikki Michalak, to name a few - with you we have built a community where our children are accepted and have opportunities to grow, work, and play. We are so much better because of you! I am eternally grateful for the families and friends we have met and grown with along this journey. Those who live and know “my world”, because of you our journey has been more entertaining, engaged and hopeful. Together we have formed friendships and made opportunities for our children to make lifelong friends, something I once thought impossible. Christina, Amanda, Michael and Austin for your support, love and laughter. Although our branches grow in different directions our roots are one. I love you all enormously!
